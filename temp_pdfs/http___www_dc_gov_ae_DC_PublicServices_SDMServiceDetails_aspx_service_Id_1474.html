1. A fee equaling to 50% of fees paid to the Court of First Instance court,  shall be charged on the challenge of the appeal against decisions issued in cases other than personal status, which should be calculated according to the following principles (1) If the appeal is retorted on the challenge with full challenged ruling, then the full payable fee is charged on the challenge against the appeal. (2) If the appeal is retorted on the challenge with a part of challenged ruling then the fees is paid on the basis of value of this part. DHS
2. If there are several appellants and the verdict was issued on them jointly or conjunctionally, then the fees will be charged once from whom had first applies for the appeal. DHS
3. . if there was multiple appellant and the judgement was not issued jointly, separate fees will be taken for each appeal, whether the appellants submitted one or multiple appeal petition. DHS
4. if the appeal was for a  judgement issued regarding a subsidiary issue which does not end the dispute and results in  stopping the lawsuit, there will be charge of 25% of the fees taken in first degree. DHS

7. Appeal against the judgment issued in personal matter cases except in relation with maintenance 100 DHS
