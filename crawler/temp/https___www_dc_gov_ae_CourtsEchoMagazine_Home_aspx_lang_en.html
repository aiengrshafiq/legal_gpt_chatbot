The Statement of His Excellency Dr. Saif Ghanem Al Suwaidi - Director-General of Dubai Courts
General Supervisor of Courts’ Echo Magazine

The power of a nation is forged in the crucible of knowledge and its dissemination. Inspired by the wisdom of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President of the State, Prime Minister, Ruler of Dubai, “May God protect him”, who proclaimed that ‘the might of the pen and knowledge far surpasses any other force’, Dubai Courts has been steadfast in its commitment to publish the “Courts’ Echo” magazine in recent times. This publication serves as a significant conduit for illuminating contemporary legal advancements and envisioning the future through comprehensive, trustworthy reports and dialogues that the magazine conducts with legal luminaries and judges, who contribute their insights and experiences in the realm of justice and law. Consequently, the “Courts’ Echo” magazine has emerged as a trusted touchstone and a principal reservoir of judicial knowledge in the Emirate of Dubai.

The augmentation of legal cognizance and the enrichment of comprehension pertaining to justice and legislation are requisites among societal members. The magazine, in collaboration with a diverse array of specialized sectors, has unveiled an extensive spectrum of judicial and legal subjects, encapsulating both contemporary and prospective challenges. We harbor the hope that the readership of the “Courts’ Echo” magazine will derive substantial benefit from this discourse and content. The significance of the issuance of the “Courts’ Echo” magazine escalates in order to actualize and disseminate this knowledge, analyses, and viewpoints to the community, a mission that our sagacious government is ardently committed to fulfilling.
