Settlements To present disputes between the parties (Civil, Commercial, Real Estate or Personal Status) before the competent authority in order resolve them amicably and in through simple procedures quickly and cost effectively without resorting to litigation Claims The first degree of litigation and allows the client to register in the proceedings of the First Instance and includes five sub-types (Civil, Commercial, Labour, Real Estate and Personal Status). Temporary and Summary judicial orders A service through which clients can submit applications for payments order for consolidated debt which has not been paid, in addition to urgent requests where there is a possibility of time lapse which does not interfere with the original rights Contestation of judgements A service provided to clients related to registration for all types of appeals, whether before the Appeal Court or Supreme Court or for seeking a petition to reconsider judgments.
